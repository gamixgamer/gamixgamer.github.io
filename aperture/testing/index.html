Frequently Asked Questions:

  1. Q: How much money can I earn from doing these tests?
    A: You can earn the value of approximately 500$ or your year's equivalent simply for participating in some of our tests and/or cake
  2. Q: How long will it take to complete these tests of yours?
    A: Test subjects are usually imported and exported within a few workdays
  3. Q: Are these tests in anyway dangerous?
    A: The Aperture Science Enrichment Center focuses on achieving goals that were once thought impossible.
    If you follow our safety protocols, Aperture Science can ensure that you will not receive any fatal injuries and/or death
    that does is not a deliberate part of any test.
  4. Q: Why hire me?
    A: The Aperture Science Enrichment Center wants the very best test subjects that science can afford and Aperture Science regulations require us to say that you are it.
